Football & Other Sports Festival

Join us for a celebration of sports, competition, and community spirit!

About the Games

The Football & Other Games Festival promotes community, healthy living, and the joy of competition. It is a celebration of teamwork, athletic excellence, and friendship.

Objectives of the Event

  • Encourage youth engagement through sports
  • Promote a healthy and active lifestyle
  • Build community spirit through fun activities
  • Recognize talents with rewards and prizes

The Football Tournament

The highlight of the event is the football tournament, with teams battling it out for glory. All are welcome—amateurs or professionals—to show their skills!

Tournament Highlights

  • Exciting knockout stages leading to the finals
  • Live commentary and entertainment
  • Grand prizes for winning teams
  • Cheerleader performances and halftime shows

Explore Other Games

Apart from football, the festival offers a variety of games and sports for everyone! Whether you prefer basketball, table tennis, or relay races, there’s something to enjoy.

Basketball
